Use of MailSift

MailSift is provided to help users organize Gmail by filtering college admissions promotional messages into a College Ads label. Users are responsible for reviewing their Gmail labels and settings to make sure messages are organized as expected.

No Guarantee

MailSift uses automated matching rules and may make mistakes. It may miss promotional emails or move messages that a user would prefer to keep in the inbox. Users should check the College Ads label and adjust protected preferences as needed.

Gmail Access

MailSift requires user-granted Gmail permission to scan and organize messages. Users may revoke access at any time through the extension or their Google Account permissions.

Acceptable Use

Users may not use MailSift to violate laws, abuse Gmail, interfere with others' accounts, or attempt to reverse engineer or misuse the extension.

Changes

MailSift may change over time as filtering rules, user preferences, and product behavior improve. Continued use of MailSift after updates means the user accepts the updated version.

Contact

For support, contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.

MailSift is an independent student project and is not affiliated with Gmail, Google, or any college or university.
