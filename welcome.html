Thanks for installing College Ads Sorter!

To protect your privacy and ensure this tool runs completely free forever, it utilizes your computer's built-in browser AI framework.

One-Time Activation Required

Because Google is still rolling this feature out, you must flip one quick developer switch in Chrome to enable the local AI core:

  1. Open a new tab and go to: chrome://flags/
  2. Search for the flag: "Prompt API for Gemini Nano"
  3. Change its dropdown setting from Default to Enabled
  4. Click the Relaunch button that pops up at the bottom of your screen.

Once Chrome restarts, open Gmail and watch your inbox clean itself!
